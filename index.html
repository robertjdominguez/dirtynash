Dirty Santa and the Nash Equilbirum

John Nash

Made cinematically famous by Russell Crowe, John Forbes Nash was an American mathematician who advanced the field of game theory. Nash's work represents the dominant strategy a player can utilize in a game.

Dirty Santa

The game goes by many names: White Elephant, Dirty Santa, Ugly Christmas, Yankee Swap, etc. Essentially, people sit around and try to make each other laugh by causing gag-gifts to be chosen by different players while simultaneously trying to get the best gift one can. The goal is to maximize the utiltiy of your gift and make the optimal selection.

The Point

In order to make things simpler, this app makes a couple of assumptions. Primarily, every gift can be stolen twice, maximum. Even if a gift is unavailable, its value is still factored into the average value of all of the gifts. If a gift worth at least the average value of all gifts is available, it should be stolen. If more than one above average is available, the highest-value gift should be selected. If none of the available gifts are equal to or above the average, one's best choice is to pick a new, unopened gift.






Instructions

To the right, you'll see your inputs. For each item in the game, include a name and a value. The value you assign to each item is completely subjective. However, it's best to keep it on a scale of 1-10, with 10 being the most valuable.
